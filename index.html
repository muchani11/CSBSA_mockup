Join UT's CSB Student Association to find your niche among your peers and enter an atmosphere of professionalism, inclusion, and fun.
The CSB Student Association (CSBSA) is the official student organization representing the Texas CSB program at the University of Texas at Austin. As such, CSBSA seeks to provide a supportive and welcoming home for all CSB students. Join us at our monthly meetings to enjoy a delicious free dinner, and hear about the plans we're cooking up!
Completely free
Membership in CSB Student Association does not cost you anything. We don't charge you for dues or other expenses (not even for dinner)! All we ask for is your participation, whether in monthly meetings, corporate meetings, or other optional events.
Find your community
This program provides a home for a wide range of students - from aspiring entrepreneurs to students interested in various fields of business and computer science. Forge new friendships and explore new hobbies!
